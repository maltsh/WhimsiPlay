Returns Policy

Effective Date: [01 October]

Return Process

At WhimsiPlay Toys, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, please review our Returns Policy to understand how we can assist you.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • We accept returns on products within 10 days from the date of purchase.
  • To be eligible for a return, the product must be in its original condition, unused, and in the same packaging as you received it.

2. Initiating a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer support team at [insert email address] or [insert phone number] to notify us of your return request.
  • Provide the following information: your order number,

the reason for the return, and whether you would like a refund or an exchange.

3. Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the return is due to an error on our part.
  • We recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method to ensure the safe return of your product.

 

4. Refunds and Exchanges

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you regarding the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If your return is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original payment method within [insert number] business days.
  • Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the requested exchange item is unavailable, we will process a refund instead.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items not in their original condition, damaged, or missing parts

6. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately, and we will arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Returns Policy, please contact us at:
